My mission

My mission in life is to make it easier for billions of people to learn English in the future
The sad reality is that the current state of English language teaching around the world is not very good.
I want to change that.
I have many ideas about how to make it easier for people to learn English, ranging from specific improvements in how grammar is taught to improvements in how schools operate.
However, the area in which I plan to have the most immediate impact is in the area of pronunciation and the International Phonetic Alphabet
